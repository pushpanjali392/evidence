ABOUT EVIDENCE

We’re investors who started out as entrepreneurs and have spent many years working in all parts of the healthcare system including working with physicians on healthcare delivery, working within government to create and implement policy, and working with payers on innovation and digital transformation. Healthcare needs innovation in order to change the trajectory of spending and to ensure access to life saving and life changing care for all. We invest in the companies driving those changes and work hand in hand with the entrepreneurs we back to help them grow. This includes customer introductions, strategy support, team building, product development, or just being an empathetic ear as you and your team surmount the inevitable challenges of building a high growth startup.
